Reagan Medical Center

Primary Care Practice, Urgent Care & Imaging Centers located in Five Forks, Lawrenceville, Dacula, Johns Creek and Suwanee, GA

At Reagan Medical Center, the primary care team provides high-quality health care to people of all ages in Lawrenceville, Dacula, Suwanee, and Johns Creek, Georgia.

Reagan Medical Center is a full-service primary care practice. Individuals and families from across the state visit Reagan Medical Center for all of their medical needs. 

The team offers everything from urgent care and pain management to imaging, blood work, and specialist referrals. Patients can also access preventive care, including Pap smears and echocardiograms.

Each satellite office is conveniently located and features state-of-the-art medical equipment.  Most locations are open 365 days a year, including on weekends and holidays. The team accepts walk-in appointments and keeps copays affordable. They set aside time for each patient, never rushing appointments.

The Reagan Medical Center staff is friendly and welcoming. The doctors, nurses, and support staff are compassionate and listen to every individual’s symptoms, concerns, and goals. They use that information to develop personalized treatment plans that optimize health and vitality.

Call the nearest Reagan Medical Center office today to schedule a new patient appointment, or book your visit online.
